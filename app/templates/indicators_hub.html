Indicator Layers

13 policy-oriented data layers: political → political-economy. Each category links to where it appears (Countries & Regions, Country Dashboard, or dedicated pages). Data feeds to be wired (World Bank, IMF, ACLED, SIPRI, etc.).

View full roadmap · Countries & Regions

🏦 1. Macroeconomic Stress Indicators

Question: “Can this country absorb shock?”

  • GDP growth (quarterly + annual)
  • Inflation
  • FX reserves
  • Current account balance
  • External debt / Debt-to-GDP
  • Sovereign credit rating
  • Bond spreads

Sources: World Bank, IMF, BIS, FRED, Trading Economics, OECD

→ Countries & Regions · Country Dashboard → Economic tab (Sovereign rating / Macro stress)

⚡ 2. Energy & Commodity Exposure

Energy moves geopolitics. Supply shock mapping.

  • Oil production
  • Gas exports / LNG capacity
  • Major pipeline routes
  • Energy import dependency %
  • Rare earth production
  • Grain export/import exposure

Overlay with sanctions, chokepoints, conflict zones

→ Country Dashboard → Economic tab (Energy & commodity)

🛡 3. Military Capability Snapshot

Open-source metrics. Escalation model has structural muscle behind it.

  • Military spending (SIPRI)
  • Active troops
  • Naval tonnage
  • Major weapons imports/exports
  • Defense alliances

SIPRI, arms trade data

→ Country Dashboard → Security tab

📈 4. Public Opinion & Legitimacy

Policy shifts follow political survival. Domestic constraint modeling.

  • Leader approval ratings
  • Protest frequency
  • Coup attempts history
  • Election cycle timing
  • Fragile state indicators

Combine with UN vote drift + sanctions pressure

→ Country Dashboard → Stability tab · Elections, approval & protests

🌐 5. Trade Flow Granularity

Not just RTAs. Sanctions friction becomes real.

  • Top 5 export destinations
  • Top 5 imports
  • Sectoral trade breakdown
  • Strategic dependence (e.g. semiconductor imports from X%)

→ Country Dashboard → Economic tab (Export dependency / Trade flow)

🧠 6. Elite & Institutional Structure

  • Central bank independence indicator
  • Party structure

→ Country Dashboard → Stability tab (Elite & institutional)

→ Country Dashboard → Stability tab (Conflict & event datasets)
