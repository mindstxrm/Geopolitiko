Higher-Order Synthesis

Synthesis is integrated across the platform. Find it in the pages where you work—no need to visit a separate synthesis hub.

Where synthesis appears

  • Situation Room — Escalation trajectory (trend + narrative), signal divergences, system state briefing link
  • Country Dashboard — Operating Picture tab (LLM situation summary + bullets), Executive One-Pager (drivers, implications)
  • Risk Engine — Escalation trajectory embedded
  • Insights — Thematic briefings (US–China, Russia–Ukraine, Middle East when you select a theme), signal divergences

Full-page views (for printing or deep dive)

  • Meta-briefing — Weekly system state
  • Escalation trajectory — Full time series + narrative
  • Cross-signal divergence — All detected mismatches
  • Executive one-pager — Per country or region (with selector)
  • Thematic: US–China · Russia–Ukraine · Middle East
  • Country operating picture: go to Countries & Regions, then open any country — first tab is Operating Picture

Synthesis requires OPENAI_API_KEY for LLM summaries. Without it, only raw data is shown.
